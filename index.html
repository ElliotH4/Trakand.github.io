SV101 — Who We Are

Founded in 2015 by the two experienced industry experts, Stephanie Son and Jung Moon, SV 101 Venture Partners is a global technology accelerator located both in the heart of Silicon Valley and Seoul, Korea. SV1 01 aspires to become a strategic partner to entrepreneurs who are determined and ambitious enough to create their global profiles.

SV 101 teams up with its strategic investment partners to invest in early-stage startups and help them seamlessly grow in the global market.

The mission of SV 101 Venture Partners is to promote innovative global startups and to incubate technology companies by providing professional accelerator programs and startup learning platforms.

  • Global Startup Eco-System Builder

  • Global Business Launch Pad

  • Bringing Innovative Technologies to Market

  • Fostering Innovation and Growth through Technology Enhancement
